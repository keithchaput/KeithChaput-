About

Hi, I’m Keith Chaput — a digital artist creating bold, accessible designs for progressive activism. Inspired by political pop art and the iconic WPA posters, my work is meant to support grassroots campaigns, policy education, and movements for justice. Everything here is free to use — because solidarity should be beautiful and accessible.
