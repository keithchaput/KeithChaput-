Overturn Citizens United

A bold visual call to end corporate influence in democracy.

Overview: This poster was created to target the political power imbalance caused by the Citizens United decision. It evokes vintage American propaganda with a modern activist tone.

Process: I used bold figures, flat shapes, and a limited palette inspired by WPA and union posters. The symbolism is sharp and immediate, intended for both digital and printed outreach.

Outcome: Shared widely online and adapted for campaign flyers, it served as a central asset in messaging around campaign finance reform.
